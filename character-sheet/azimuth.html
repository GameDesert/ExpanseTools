Character Sheet

Please read these instructions to the end before performing them.

Step 1:

Download the character sheet PDF by clicking the button below.

Download Character Sheet

Step 2:

Print the sheet on an A4 piece of paper. This can be done in either colour or greyscale, but not black-and-white. Borderless printing is preferred, but it won't affect the layout of the sheet if you don't, just make sure the sheet fills the whole A4 page.

Step 3:

Fold the sheet lengthways, so that the two inner pages are back-to-back with the front and back (inventory page) covers. Glue these blank sides together to form a booklet.

Step 4:

Fold the booklet sideways, so that the inner pages face each other. Do not glue these pages together.

Step 5:

Fill in your character's biographical information such as name, age, height, and weight on the front cover. You can also optionally add an image of your character on the first inner page.
